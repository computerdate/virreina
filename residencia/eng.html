Both residencies are aimed at artists, writers, curators, cultural managers, filmmakers, architects, and/or designers whose practice has a critical commitment to and towards the cultural sector in which it is developed. The purpose of both residencies is to encourage exploration and collective doubt, offering for four (4) weeks a moment and space to rethink the possibilities of exhibition, circulation, production, and development of material and theoretical projects while immersed in an experience tailored to the local cultural ecosystem.
